Potential Destinations

Good Angle Golf offers access to some of the best winter golf destinations in the country, from the iconic Ocean Course at Kiawah Island to the dramatic terrain of Streamsong in Florida. We offer your members the chance to experience championship-caliber golf, luxurious accommodations, and curated group itineraries at multiple destinations. Whether you're looking for oceanfront fairways, desert vistas, or pine-lined parkland classics, there's a perfect destination for every club and member group. We tailor each trip to your members' preferences, with hand-picked courses, tee times, and off-course experiences included.

Streamsong

Bowling Green, FL

Play three Top 100 courses in one trip at this elite, all-golf, all-inclusive resort. Pure golf experience, remote setting, unforgettable for members.

Cabot Citrus Farms

Brooksville, FL

Be among the first to experience Cabot's Florida masterpiece. Bold new designs, big vision, and a fresh take on the private club trip — just outside Tampa.

TPC Sawgrass

Ponte Vedra Beach, FL

Give your members the thrill of walking in Tour footsteps at this iconic Florida destination. Yes, they’ll play the island green at 17.

Trump National Doral

Miami, FL

The Blue Monster and three other tracks make Doral a Miami hotspot for luxury and energy-packed golf trips.

Innisbrook

Palm Harbor, FL

Valspar’s Copperhead Course anchors this laid-back, member-friendly resort. Great value, great golf, and easy logistics make it an ideal trip starter.

PGA National

Palm Beach, FL

Five championship courses including The Champion, home of The Bear Trap. An easy win for winter golf with strong amenities and member appeal.

Sea Island

Sea Island, GA

Luxury coastal resort with five-star service, oceanfront accommodations, and world-class golf. Perfect for high-end member trips with top-tier service.

Kiawah Island

Kiawah Island, SC

Home to the legendary Ocean Course, Kiawah blends coastal beauty with big-time golf. A must-play destination for serious member golf experiences.

We-Ko-Pa Resort

Scottsdale, AZ

Desert golf done right. Two award-winning courses, open tee sheets, and epic views make this tribal resort a hidden gem for smooth group trips.

TPC Scottsdale

Scottsdale, AZ

Let your members play the Stadium Course just like the pros. Pair it with desert views, great lodging, and Scottsdale’s lively off-course scene.

PGA Frisco

Frisco, TX

The PGA of America’s new HQ features two elite courses and a stunning new Omni resort. Your members will love this fresh, modern destination.

Omni Barton Creek

Austin, TX

Four distinct courses from Fazio, Crenshaw and Palmer meet Texas Hill Country luxury. Great mix of golf, spa, and downtown Austin nightlife.
