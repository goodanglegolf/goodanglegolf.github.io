Good Angle Golf

Private Club Member Trips, Done Right

Good Angle Golf designs and delivers high-end golf trips for private club members, hosted by their club professionals. We handle the trip planning, tee times, budgets and logistics — so you don’t have to.

Why Club Pros Choose Good Angle Golf

  • Pro-Led, Member-Focused We build trips around you and your members. Your presence makes the trip special — we make it effortless.
  • Complimentary Pro Travel Your travel is fully covered — no cost to you, and no expectation to “sell” the trip. Just invite, show up, and enjoy.
  • Easy to Launch We offer turnkey trips to top destinations like Kiawah Island, Streamsong, Sea Island, and Scottsdale — with tee times, lodging, and optional group dinners arranged.
  • Year-Round Member Engagement We strategically schedule the member trips for winter months (January through April) to keep members connected and engaged when golf at home is on pause.
  • Custom Branding Trip itineraries, emails, and signage carry your club’s name — not ours.
  • Full-Service Planning Support We handle all logistics — tee times, lodging, itineraries, and vendor coordination — so you can focus on your members. You manage communications and collect payments directly, maintaining the club-member connection.
  • Member Facing Materials We will provide you with everything you need to market and update your members about the trip. This includes a brochure and microsite promoting the trip, and a complete information packet when it's time to travel.

Why Members Love It

  • Access to premier destinations and tee times
  • Trip experiences that feel like a VIP getaway
  • Camaraderie and fun competition with fellow members and their pro
  • Keeps their golf season alive during the winter
  • No hassle, carefree logistics

Potential Destinations

Good Angle Golf offers access to some of the best winter golf destinations in the country, from the iconic Ocean Course at Kiawah Island to the dramatic terrain of Streamsong in Florida. Members can experience championship-caliber golf, luxurious accommodations, and curated group itineraries at resorts like Sea Island, PGA National, and Bandon Dunes. Whether you're looking for oceanfront fairways, desert vistas, or pine-lined parkland classics, there's a perfect destination for every club and member group. Each trip is tailored to your members' preferences, with hand-picked courses, tee times, and off-course experiences included.

Streamsong

Play three Top 100 courses in one trip at this elite, all-golf, all-inclusive resort. Pure golf experience, remote setting, unforgettable for members.

Sea Island

Luxury coastal resort with five-star service, oceanfront accommodations, and world-class golf. Perfect for high-end member trips with top-tier service.

Kiawah Island

Home to the legendary Ocean Course, Kiawah blends coastal beauty with big-time golf. A must-play destination for serious member golf experiences.

TPC Sawgrass

Jacksonville, FL

Give your members the thrill of walking in Tour footsteps at this iconic Florida destination. Yes, they’ll play the island green at 17.

Trump Doral

The Blue Monster and three other tracks make Doral a Miami hotspot for luxury and energy-packed golf trips.

Innisbrook

Valspar’s Copperhead Course anchors this laid-back, member-friendly resort. Great value, great golf, and easy logistics make it an ideal trip starter.

PGA National

Five championship courses including The Champion, home of The Bear Trap. An easy win for winter golf with strong amenities and member appeal.

TPC Frisco

The PGA of America’s new HQ features two elite courses and a stunning new Omni resort. Your members will love this fresh, modern destination..

We-Ko-Pa Resort

Desert golf done right. Two award-winning courses, open tee sheets, and epic views make this tribal resort a hidden gem for smooth group trips.

TPC Scottsdale

Let your members play the Stadium Course just like the pros. Pair it with desert views, great lodging, and Scottsdale’s lively off-course scene.

Omni Barton Creek

Four distinct courses from Fazio, Crenshaw and Palmer meet Texas Hill Country luxury. Great mix of golf, spa, and downtown Austin nightlife.

Cabot Citrus Farms

Be among the first to experience Cabot's Florida masterpiece. Bold new designs, big vision, and a fresh take on the private club trip — just outside Tampa.
